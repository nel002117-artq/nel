Policy

Readable

AF SECURITY ("we", "our", "us") provides this Android app to present company information and utility tools.

Who we are

  • AF SECURITY Email: support@afsecurity.org • Phone: +212780363315

Data we collect in the app

  • No account required This app does not require account creation and does not collect personal profile data inside the app.
  • Tool inputs stay on-device Tool inputs (estimators/converters) are processed locally on the device.

Permissions and device access

  • No sensitive runtime permissions The app does not request sensitive runtime permissions (such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location).
  • System actions only The app uses system actions to open your phone dialer and email app when you press Call or Email.

When you contact us

If you call or email us, we receive the information you choose to provide (for example: phone number, email address, and message content) so we can respond to your request.

Third parties

  • No ads / no tracking SDKs This app version does not include in-app advertising SDKs or in-app analytics/tracking SDKs.
  • External apps have their own policies If you use external apps (phone, email, app store), those services process data under their own privacy policies.

Data sharing

  • No selling of data We do not sell your personal data.
  • Support-only use We only use contact information you provide to communicate with you and handle your request.

Data retention

The app itself does not store user tool inputs on our servers. If you contact us, we may keep correspondence records as needed for support, legal, or business record purposes.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through the app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app features or legal requirements change. Any update will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@afsecurity.org or +212780363315.
